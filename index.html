About our Stone Crusher

Our stone crusher is a type of pulverizer that is used to compress or break apart a variety of large stone. Stone crushers typically feature a large crushing rate and high yield, and while they may be used as primary crushers, they are also often used as secondary or tertiary crushers. Industries that utilize stone crushers include transportation, energy, petrochemical, industrial manufacturing and smelting.
